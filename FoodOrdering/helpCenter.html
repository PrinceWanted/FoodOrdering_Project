Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Wassally?

FoodDelivery is the #1 online food ordering service in Egypt. We offer the quickest, easiest, most reliable food ordering experience. Our service helps customers find restaurants in their area, filter by cuisine, browse menus, and place their orders. Customers use FoodDelivery through our iOS and Android applications or through our website. FoodDelivery was founded in 1999 and has helped millions of Egyptians order their food in 30 seconds or less. Our customers enjoy exclusive offers from thousands of restaurants in more than 25 cities in Egypt.

How do i create an account?

Go through our main website page (home) and youll find on the top (account) click on it and then click on signup and thats it!

How can i pay online?

Online payment is available on selected restaurants. These restaurants are identified with a credit card icon on the restaurant listing. You can also filter to view online payment available restaurants by clicking on the filter tab and selecting "Online Payment Available. You will be able to choose the online payment option from participating restaurants as part of the checkout process. The payment selection screen appears after confirming the address and before the final order confirmation screen.

How do i cancel an order?

The best way to cancel your order is to contact customer care through our live chat, so we can forward your cancelation request to the restaurant.

What types of credit cards does Food Delivery accept?

Wassally accepts Master Card and Visa. Some banks' debit cards are also usable for online transactions. Please check with your bank for availability. Some credit cards issued outside of Egypt may not be usable due to fraud protection measures.

Can i make more than one order at a time?

Yes you can order from several restaurants in the same time, delivery time and fees will differ from a restaurant to another. And make sure that ordering process is completed from each restaurant

Does FoodDelivery automatically deduct from my card even if the order isn't completed?

Banks may place a temporary credit hold to your card in the amount of 20 EGP to check that the card is active and working. This hold is a standard banking procedure. It is temporary and the full amount will be released back to your account within 72 hours.

My card has been frozen. It was working before. Why did this happen?

If you try to pay online and you used more than a card or used the same card several times in a short period, system will automatically freeze your account as a security measure.

How can i get in touch with your customer care team?

You can reach our customer care through live chat on the Wassally website or by sending an email to feedback@wassally.com

How can you calculate the delivery fee?

All delivery fees posted on Wassally are provided by the restaurants and are not decided by FoodDelivery. FoodDelivery does not charge any additional delivery fees.
